ABOUT US

Serving with Care, Light, and Purpose

Looking to elevate your curb appeal with professional lawn care and landscape lighting in Dallas, TX? At Higher Ground Lawn Care & Landscape Lighting, we believe lifes better outside. Our mission is simple: to serve one another and our clients with care, to nurture healthy outdoor spaces, and to glorify the Lord who created it all. Were here to bring joy, beauty, and satisfaction to every family we serveone lawn, one landscape, and one light at a time.

Founded out of a desire to create something meaningful, Higher Ground was born when our founder, JP, decided to step away from the corporate world and build a purpose-driven business. Inspired by barefoot summers spent on his grandfathers lush lawn, JP envisioned a company that would prioritize organic practices, environmental responsibility, and a higher calling to serve others.

Today, we operate with the same passion:

  • Focusing on soil health and organic methods to protect kids, pets, and the environment.
  • Serving every property as if it were our own
  • Encouraging families and communities to spend more time together outdoors.
  • Supporting missions like 4MUS to strengthen faith, brotherhood, and family.

Lawn Care

Aeration

Revitalize tired, compacted lawns with professional core aeration services. Our process relieves soil compaction, improves water & absorption, and promotes deeper root growth, resulting in healthier, thicker turf.

Lawn Fertilization
